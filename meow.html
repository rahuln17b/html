Stranger Things

Stranger Things is a science fiction horror drama television series set in the 1980s. The series blends supernatural events with human experiences, primarily focusing on a group of young friends.
The show starts when a young boy named Will goes missing under mysterious circumstances, leading his friends and family to uncover a series of strange events in their town.
In the show, a girl with telekinetic abilities known only as Eleven becomes a central character who helps the group as they face a government conspiracy and otherworldly creatures.

The Upside Down is a key element in Stranger Things, representing a dark, parallel universe that poses a grave threat to the real world.
